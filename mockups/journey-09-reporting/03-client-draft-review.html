Cloudvault Technologies Inc. · Jan 1 – Dec 31, 2025 · Version 3

I. Independent Service Auditor's Report

We have examined Cloudvault Technologies Inc.'s description of its cloud infrastructure management system throughout the period January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025 and the suitability of the design and operating effectiveness of controls described therein, based on the criteria for a description of a service organization's system in DC section 200 and the applicable trust services criteria.

II. Management's Assertion

Cloudvault Technologies Inc.'s management has provided its assertion that the accompanying description fairly presents the system that was designed and implemented throughout the period January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025. Management further asserts that the controls within the system were suitably designed and operated effectively1 throughout the specified period to meet the applicable trust services criteria.

V. Results of Tests

Our testing of 14 controls across the trust services criteria categories identified 12 controls operating effectively throughout the examination period. Two exceptions were noted:

Exception 1 (CC6.2 — Logical Access): In 3 of 25 sampled user access reviews, the quarterly review was completed 5–8 days past the defined policy window2. Management implemented an automated reminder system in July 2025, and no late reviews occurred in Q3 or Q4 2025.

Exception 2 (CC7.1 — System Operations Monitoring): One critical monitoring alert rule was inadvertently disabled for 11 days during a platform migration in March 20253. The gap was detected during an internal security review and remediated with additional change management controls for monitoring configurations.
